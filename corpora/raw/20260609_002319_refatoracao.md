### 📦 1.3 signals/ — Normalização de Sinais

**Arquivos criados:**
- `invest_os/signals/base.py` (79 linhas)
- `invest_os/signals/__init__.py`

**Tipos de sinal:**
- `MarketSignal` — dados on-chain (market_cap, transaction_volume, realized_cap)
- `SocialSignal` — comunidade (community_size, governance_participation)
- `CodeSignal` — dev activity (github_stars, github_forks, contributors)
- `GovernanceSignal` — maturidade DAO (has_sbt, voting_enabled, has_oracles)

**Pipeline de normalização:** `normalize(signal)` → `NormalizedSignal` (valor 0-1, confidence)
- `normalize_market()` → baseado em NVT/MVRV normalizados
- `normalize_social()` → weighted (40% size, 60% participation)
- `normalize_code()` → weighted (40% stars, 30% forks, 30% contributors)
- `normalize_governance()` → 35% SBT, 35% voting, 30% oracles
